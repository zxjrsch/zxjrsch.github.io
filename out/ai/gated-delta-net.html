Gated Delta Net and Mamba
Apr 11 by Xue J. Zhao
Sequence modelling is being re-thought along three direction: linear attention, test time training, and state space models. The criteria to evaluate emerging approaches on a theoretical basis include long context complexity and the degree to which a new approach can preserve and improve the essential properties already present in quadratic softmax attention, such as in context learning. At the systems level, evaluation mainly concerns scability through the ability to parallelize model operations for efficient computation on tensor core hardware. The following is a high level description of some proposals from each of the three camps.
